important charachters

Thomas is a teenaged boy who enters the Glade with no memories other than of his first name. Thomas is inquisitive and quick-witted. He often rejects authority, and he shows disdain for the condescending “Greenie” label he earns on his arrival. He was about 16 years old when he enters the Glade. And he is played by the actor Dylan Obrien

Newt is the Second-in-Command of the Gladers.He has long blond hair and a heavy limp from attempting suicide when he was a Runner. He was taller than Alby, despite the one year age difference. Newt was named after Isaac Newton. And he is played by the actor Thomas Brodie

Minho is first introduced when he and Ben return from the Maze, running past Thomas and Chuck. Is the Keeper of Runners. He goes into the maze everyday with other runners to search for a way out. He's been there for almost two years. And is played by the actor Ki Hong Lee.
